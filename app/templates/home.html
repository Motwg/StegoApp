Dear User,

This webpage was created in order to show how can you hide some message called "watermark" in almost any picture using different steganography algorithms and compare their resistance on some chosen attacks, which might change or even erase your watermark from the image.


How to use the app?

Step 1

In the first section find text field called "Watermark" 1. Type your message here, which you wish to hide in an image shown above 3. There is also a possibility to chose your own image from disk - just press the button with an arrow pointing up 2 and chose any picture with jpg or png extension. After successful upload of the file your image should appear above 3.

Step 2

Now is time to choose one of implemented steganography algorithms. Find a field "Algorithm" 1 and chose which one would you like to use. Depending on your choice different parameters may be shown in the panel 3. One of them - "Channel" is shared by them and represents in which channel in the image (red, green or blue) algorithm should embed your watermark. You can also change other parameters as you wish. When you are ready press "Embedding" button 2 and wait until a loading bar fills up.

Step 3

After disappearing of the loading bar your image with hidden watermark should appear in second section 1. It is available to download by pressing the button with an arrow pointing down 2. There is also functionality called steganalysis, which can check every channel in your image for watermarks thanks to build in neural network. You can perform steganalysis via "Steganalysis" button 3, then a message should pop up with network prediction and assurance for each channel. When you are ready proceed to the field "Type of attack" 4 and chose any attack to apply to your image. By default when you uploaded your image it was converted to png extension. That means an attack "PNG conversion" would not change the image in any way - just like there is no attack. In order to continue press "Perform attack" button 4 and wait until a loading bar fills up.

Step 4

After disappearing of the loading bar your modified image 1 will show up. Then use the same algorithm 3 and parameters 5 as in Step 2 (or another if you know what to do) and press "Extract" button 4. A loading bar should pop up and it may take some time. Finally, when the process of extracting is completed your message (or rather what's left from your message depending of chosen attack and algorithm) should appear in the field 6 on the left.
Your message might be duplicated due to the fact that implemented algorithms embed watermark many times in an image in order to resist an attack.
There is also a possibility to chose your own image from disk and skip the whole process of attacking an image - just press the button with an arrow pointing up 2 and chose any picture with jpg or png extension.

Let's begin!
